Why We Play: About KW Cares

KW Cares is a 501(c)(3) charity created to support Keller Williams associates and their families during times of extreme hardship. Whether recovering from a natural disaster or facing an unforeseen medical emergency, KW Cares is the heart of our culture in action—agents helping agents when they need it most.

Every dollar raised from this tournament goes directly to our local fund, ensuring we can always take care of our own right here in the Greater Des Moines area.
